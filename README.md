# Med Locker - AI-Powered Digital Health Passport

A modern, visually stunning, and fully responsive digital health management platform powered by AI.

## 🌟 Features

### Landing Page
- **Hero Section** with tagline "Your Health, Secured & Smart"
- **4 Feature Cards**: AI Health Monitoring, Secure Records, Appointment Tracker, Health Analytics
- **Testimonials** with Indian healthcare professionals and patients
- **Professional Footer** with links and social media icons

### Authentication
- **Login/Signup** with email and password
- **Role Selection**: Patient, Doctor, or Admin
- **Social Login** options (Google, Facebook)
- **Forgot Password** functionality
- Complete signup form with validation

### Dashboard
Comprehensive sidebar navigation with:
- Dashboard Home
- Health Records
- Appointments
- Analytics
- AI Insights
- AI Chat Bot
- Medicine Reminder
- Settings

### Dashboard Home
- **Health Summary Widgets**: Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, Blood Sugar, Temperature
- **Upcoming Appointments** with Indian doctor names
- **AI Recommendations** with priority badges
- **Medicine Reminder Summary**
- **Health Goals Progress** bars
- **Quick Actions**: Add Record, Schedule Appointment, Upload Lab Results, Upload Prescription

### Health Records
- **Searchable Table/List** with filters by Date, Type, Doctor
- **Expandable Details** for each record
- **Upload New Records** (PDF/images)
- **AI-Generated Insights** for each record
- Indian doctor names throughout

### AI Insights & Analytics
- **Interactive Charts**: Weight trends, Blood Pressure, Blood Sugar, Heart Rate
- Built with **Recharts** library
- **AI Tips and Alerts** with priority levels
- **Exportable Reports**
- **Health Score Cards** with progress indicators
- **AI Predictions** with confidence levels

### Appointments
- **Upcoming/Past Appointments** tabs
- **Book Appointment** with calendar UI
- **AI Suggestions** for scheduling
- **Video/In-person** appointment types
- **Reschedule/Cancel** functionality
- Indian doctor names with avatars

### AI Chat Bot
- **Interactive Chat Interface** for symptoms and health questions
- **Friendly, Cautious AI Responses** ("You may have...", "It could be...")
- **Medical Disclaimer** on every response: "This is not medical advice. Please consult your doctor."
- **Attach Reports/Images** option
- **Chat History Panel**
- **Quick Suggestions** for common questions
- **Animated Bot Avatar**

### Medicine Reminder
- **Upload Prescription** (image/PDF)
- **Auto-Generate Medication Schedule** with AI
- **Morning/Afternoon/Night** timing
- **Empty/Full Stomach** indicators
- **Dosage Information**
- **Interactive Reminders** with notifications
- **Snooze** and **Mark as Taken** buttons
- **Next Dose Countdown**
- **Attractive Icons** for pills and schedule
- **Progress Tracking** for each medication

### Settings
- **Profile Management**: Edit personal information, profile picture
- **Security**: Change password, 2FA, active sessions
- **Notifications**: Customize alerts for appointments, medications, health alerts
- **Privacy**: Data encryption status, download data, data deletion options
